"A LOVELY GREEK SALAD RECIPE"

A Greek Salad is a fresh, vibrant dish that perfectly captures the flavors of the Mediterranean. Made with crisp cucumbers, juicy tomatoes, red onions, briny Kalamata olives, and creamy feta cheese, itâ€™s both refreshing and satisfying. The salad is usually dressed with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil, oregano, and a touch of lemon juice, giving it a simple yet zesty flavor. Light, colorful, and nutrient-packed, Greek Salad is ideal as a healthy side dish or even a main meal on warm days. Its balance of tangy, salty, and fresh flavors makes it a timeless favorite in Greek cuisine and a delicious way to enjoy wholesome ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 large cucumbers, sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup Kalamata olives
  • 200g feta, cubed
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Combine tomatoes, cucumbers, red onion, and green pepper in a bowl.
  2. Add olives and feta cubes.
  3. Whisk olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Toss salad with dressing and serve immediately.
