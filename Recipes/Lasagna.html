"THE PERECT LASAGNA RECIPE"

Lasagna is a hearty Italian classic made by layering sheets of pasta with a rich tomato-based meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and plenty of melted cheese. Each layer soaks in the flavors of garlic, herbs, and seasoning, creating a comforting dish that is both filling and flavorful. Baked in the oven until golden and bubbly on top, lasagna is perfect for family dinners, gatherings, or even meal prepping for the week. What makes this recipe special is the balance between the savory meat sauce and the creamy, cheesy layers. The slow-simmered sauce brings depth and richness, while the melted mozzarella and parmesan add that irresistible gooey texture. Whether served with a crisp green salad or warm garlic bread, this lasagna is sure to impress and leave everyone asking for seconds.

INGREDIENTS

  • Lasagna noodles – 12 sheets (pre-cooked or oven-ready)
  • Ground beef or ground pork – 500g (about 1 lb)
  • Olive oil – 2 tablespoons
  • Onion – 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Garlic – 3 cloves, minced
  • Tomato sauce – 2 cups (or 1 large can)
  • Tomato paste – 2 tablespoons
  • Crushed tomatoes – 1 cup
  • Italian seasoning (oregano, basil, thyme) – 2 teaspoons
  • Salt & black pepper – to taste
  • Ricotta cheese – 1 ½ cups (or cottage cheese as a substitute)
  • Mozzarella cheese – 2 cups, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese – ½ cup, grated
  • Egg – 1 large (to bind the ricotta mixture)
  • Fresh parsley – 2 tablespoons, chopped (optional, for garnish)

STEPS

  1. Meat Sauce Mixture
  2. 500g (1 lb) ground beef or pork
  3. 2 tbsp olive oil
  4. 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  5. 3 cloves garlic, minced
  6. 2 cups tomato sauce
  7. 2 tbsp tomato paste
  8. 1 cup crushed tomatoes
  9. 2 tbsp Italian seasoning (oregano,basil,thyme)
  10. Salt & Black Pepper to taste
